Location Based Mobile Model Revenue Analysis

The analysis of mobile phone sales in several key cities of India provides valuable insights into the dynamics of the mobile
retail market. This dataset reveals distinct patterns and preferences among consumers in different locations. For instance,
in Ahmedabad, the iPhone 13 commands the highest selling price at 1,001,870 INR, indicating a strong demand for the latest
model in the city. Conversely, Gandhinagar favors the iPhone 12, with a selling price of 791,574 INR, suggesting a specific
consumer preference for this model. In contrast, Rajkot, Surat, and Vadodara show a shared affinity for the iPhone 12 Mini,
with selling prices ranging from 955,892 INR to 1,389,197 INR. These variations underscore the importance of considering
local market dynamics when pricing and marketing mobile devices. This analysis not only offers a snapshot of the mobile
retail landscape but also paves the way for strategic decision-making, helping businesses tailor their approach to meet
the unique demands of consumers in each city.
