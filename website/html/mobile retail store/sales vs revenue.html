Top Mobile Brands in Gujarat: Sales Volume and Revenue Analysis

Based on the analysis of sales data from Gujarat's top four cities over the period from
Q3 2022 to Q3 2023, it is evident that the total market revenue amounted to an
impressive ₹71,371,313. The data reveals that Apple emerged as the top-selling brand
in terms of sales revenue, generating a remarkable ₹17,896,452 in revenue during
the given time frame. On the other hand, OnePlus stands out as the leader in terms
of quantity sold, amassing a considerable revenue of ₹6,876,980.

The sales figures indicate a strong consumer demand for tech products, with Apple's
popularity highlighting the continued preference for its premium devices. OnePlus,
on the other hand, appears to have secured a significant market share with its
competitive pricing and attractive features, appealing to a wide range of consumers.
