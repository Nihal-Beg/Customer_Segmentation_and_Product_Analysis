Location Based Mobile Brands Analysis

Based on the comprehensive analysis of top-selling mobile phone brands across four major cities in Gujarat, several key insights have
emerged. Firstly, when considering sales volume, it is evident that Rajkot leads the pack, with the prominent brand being OnePlus.
This indicates a significant preference for OnePlus smartphones among the residents of Rajkot. Following closely in terms of sales
volume is Vadodara, where Samsung is the favored mobile brand of choice.

However, the analysis takes an intriguing turn when focusing on revenue generation. Vadodara emerges as the city with the highest
revenue generated from mobile phone sales, thanks to the strong presence of Apple products. Furthermore, Surat also stands out
as a city where Apple products are contributing significantly to the overall revenue, securing its position as the second-highest
revenue-generating city. This analysis underscores the nuanced dynamics of the mobile phone market in Gujarat, showcasing regional
preferences in both sales volume and revenue. While OnePlus and Samsung enjoy popularity in terms of sales volume, Apple's premium
offerings appear to dominate the revenue charts in Vadodara and Surat. These findings provide valuable insights for mobile phone
manufacturers and retailers looking to tailor their strategies to the unique preferences of each city's consumer base.
