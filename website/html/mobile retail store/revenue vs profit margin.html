Top Mobile Brands in Gujarat: Revenue and Profitability Analysis

The analysis of sales data from Gujarat's top four cities for the period of Q3 2022 to Q3 2023 reveals some
interesting insights. During this time frame, the total market revenue in these cities amounted to an impressive
₹71,371,313. Among all the brands,Apple emerged as the clear leader in terms of sales, generating a substantial
revenue of ₹17,896,452. On the other hand, Samsung proved to be the most profitable brand, boasting a remarkable
profit margin of 12.53%.

The data highlights the strong performance of both Apple and Samsung in the market, with Apple dominating
sales and Samsung excelling in profit margins. It is evident that consumers in these cities have a preference
for Apple products, contributing significantly to their sales figures. Samsung, on the other hand, has managed
to maintain a healthy profit margin, indicating efficient cost management and pricing strategies..
