Home Decor and Furniture Profitability Analysis

In this analysis of furniture items, their respective selling prices in INR, and the associated profits, we gain insights into the
financial dynamics of the furniture industry. The Wardrobe leads with a selling price of 1,754,447 INR, generating a profit
of 397,783 INR, reflecting its popularity and profitability. The Recliner follows closely, priced at 1,557,766 INR, with a substantial
profit of 365,854 INR, indicating its appeal among customers. Dining Tables, Sofas, and Study Desks, with selling prices of
1,421,913 INR, 1,387,455 INR, and 1,379,974 INR, respectively, each generate healthy profits, highlighting the overall financial
stability and demand within the furniture market. This analysis underscores the relationship between pricing and profitability,
guiding businesses in their strategies and customers in their choices within the furniture industry.
