Flipkart Revenue VS Review Analysis

In the analysis of the dataset comparing Flipkart prices and review ratings, it is evident that there exists a very weak
positive correlation, as indicated by a correlation coefficient of 0.0362. This suggests that, to a minimal extent,
higher-priced products on Flipkart may be associated with slightly higher review ratings, and vice versa. However,
it's crucial to emphasize that this correlation is extremely weak, almost negligible. In practical terms, it means that
the price of a product on Flipkart has very little influence on the review ratings, and vice versa. Other factors, such as
product quality, brand reputation, and customer preferences, likely play a far more significant role in determining the
review ratings on the platform. Therefore, while this analysis highlights a slight trend, it is not a reliable predictor of
review ratings on Flipkart, and consumers should consider various factors when making purchasing decisions.
