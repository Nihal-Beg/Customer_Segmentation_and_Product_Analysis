Top Selling Laptop Brands Comparison Revenue Analysis (Amazon & Flipkart)

In this comparative analysis of laptop prices between Amazon and Flipkart, it is evident that there are significant variations
in the pricing strategies of these two prominent e-commerce platforms. Samsung emerges as the brand with the highest average
laptop price on Amazon, commanding an average of 15,171,174 Indian Rupees per unit. Meanwhile, on Flipkart, Samsung laptops
are priced significantly lower, averaging 8,378,688 INR per unit. This discrepancy highlights the dynamic nature of pricing in the online
retail market, with brands often adjusting their prices to remain competitive on different platforms. Apple laptops also show noteworthy
differences in pricing, with an average price of 14,052,316 INR on Amazon, making them one of the higher-priced options. On Flipkart,
Apple laptops are still premium but relatively more affordable, averaging 6,328,633 INR per unit. This contrast reflects the diverse pricing
strategies adopted by brands across e-commerce platforms, potentially catering to different customer segments based on their willingness
to spend.< Dell and HP maintain competitive pricing on both platforms, but with slightly higher average prices on Amazon.

Microsoft laptops also follow this pattern, with higher prices on Amazon compared to Flipkart. Acer and Asus showcase similar pricing
strategies on both platforms, while Sony and Toshiba offer laptops at more budget-friendly prices.
