Top Selling Laptop Brand E-Commerce Revenue and Sales Volume Analysis

In this analysis, we have examined the prices and quantities of laptops from various popular brands in the Indian market.
Samsung emerges as the brand with the highest average laptop price, commanding an impressive 15,171,174 Indian Rupees
per unit, while also maintaining a substantial quantity of 168 laptops. Dell closely follows with an average price of 13,880,886 INR
and 154 laptops in stock. Acer, HP, and Microsoft exhibit similar pricing, hovering around the 13 million INR mark, with quantities
of 153 units each. Apple, known for its premium products, maintains a slightly higher average price of 14,052,316 INR for 152
laptops. Asus and Lenovo offer laptops at more competitive prices, averaging around 13 million INR, with 149 units each in stock.
Sony and Toshiba, on the other hand, offer more budget-friendly options, with Sony averaging 12,951,542 INR for 145 laptops,
while Toshiba is the most affordable at 11,051,051 INR for 124 units. This analysis provides valuable insights into the pricing strategies
and market presence of these laptop brands, catering to a diverse range of consumers with varying budgets and preferences.
