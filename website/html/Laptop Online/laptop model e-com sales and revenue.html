Top Selling Laptop Model E-Commerce Revenue and Sales Volume Analysis

In this additional analysis, we delve deeper into the specific laptop models offered by these brands, along with their
respective prices and quantities. Sony VAIO leads the pack in terms of price, with its VAIO model commanding
12,951,542 Indian Rupees per unit, while having 145 laptops in stock. Samsung offers two models, the Galaxy Book
and the Notebook, with prices averaging around 7.4 million INR and quantities of 86 and 82 units, respectively.
Apple's MacBook Air and MacBook Pro models come at competitive prices of 7.2 million INR and 6.8 million INR,
with quantities of 80 and 72 laptops available. Microsoft offers both the Surface Laptop and Surface Book, with
prices around 6.7 million INR and 6.6 million INR and quantities of 79 and 74 units, respectively. Toshiba presents
the Satellite and Portege models at more budget-friendly prices averaging 5.7 million INR and 5.3 million INR,
with quantities of 66 and 58 units. HP's EliteBook, known for its performance and reliability, comes in at an average
price of 4.3 million INR with 48 units available.
