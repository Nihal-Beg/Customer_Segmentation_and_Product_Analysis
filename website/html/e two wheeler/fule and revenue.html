Electric Two Wheeler Models Range and Revenue Analysis

In this comprehensive analysis of electric two-wheeler models, we evaluate their respective prices in INR alongside their
battery range in kilometers, providing valuable insights for potential buyers seeking a balance between cost and performance.
The TVS iQube stands out with the highest price tag at INR 9,377,554, paired with an impressive battery range of approximately
110.91 km, catering to riders looking for an extended range in their electric scooter. The Hero Electric Optima and Bajaj Chetak
Electric follow closely, offering competitive pricing at INR 9,061,250 and INR 8,543,904, respectively, and similar battery ranges
of approximately 110.17 km and 106.68 km, making them attractive choices for daily commuting. The Ather 450X, with a price
of INR 8,177,173, provides a slightly lower battery range of around 105.53 km, emphasizing performance and innovation. The
Revolt RV400, while commanding a premium price at INR 9,931,626, offers a commendable battery range of approximately
103.76 km, targeting riders who prioritize sustainability and affordability. This analysis empowers consumers to make informed
decisions based on their preferences for price, battery range, and overall value within the electric two-wheeler market.
