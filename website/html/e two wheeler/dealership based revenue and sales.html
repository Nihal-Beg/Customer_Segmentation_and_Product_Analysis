Dealership Based Electric Two Wheeler Models Sales Volume and Revenue Analysis

In this analysis of dealerships offering electric two-wheeler models and their respective prices in INR, we encounter
a variety of choices for consumers interested in eco-friendly transportation. The Ather Experience Center presents
the Bajaj Chetak Electric at a price of INR 2,930,551, providing a premium option for those seeking style and sustainability.
The Bajaj EV Dealership offers the TVS iQube at INR 2,717,588, targeting urban commuters in search of an efficient
and cost-effective electric scooter. The Hero Electric Showroom showcases the Hero Electric Optima at INR 2,520,597,
emphasizing practicality and affordability. Finally, the TVS Electric Dealer introduces the Revolt RV400 at INR 2,838,339,
appealing to riders interested in a blend of performance and sustainability. This analysis highlights the diverse offerings
from these dealerships, enabling customers to find an electric two-wheeler that aligns with their preferences and
budget while contributing to a greener future.
