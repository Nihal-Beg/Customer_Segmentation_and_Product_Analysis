Electric Two Wheeler Models Sales Volume and Revenue Analysis

In this analysis of electric two-wheeler models, their respective prices in INR, and available quantities, we observe
a range of eco-friendly options for environmentally conscious consumers. The Revolt RV400 leads the pack in terms
of price, with a tag of INR 9,931,626, and offers 102 units, targeting those looking for a premium electric riding experience.
The TVS iQube and Hero Electric Optima provide competitive alternatives, priced at INR 9,377,554 and INR 9,061,250,
respectively, and each offering more than 100 units, making them attractive choices for urban commuters seeking affordability
and efficiency. The Bajaj Chetak Electric, priced at INR 8,543,904, combines style with practicality and offers 95 units, catering
to riders looking for a blend of convenience and eco-consciousness. The Ather 450X, with a price tag of INR 8,177,173, presents
a compelling option, providing 89 units for those interested in performance and innovation. This analysis showcases a variety
of electric two-wheeler models, accommodating different budgets and preferences within the rapidly evolving electric vehicle market.
