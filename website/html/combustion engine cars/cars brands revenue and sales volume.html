Top Car Brands Revenue and Sales Volume Analysis

In the analysis of car brands and their corresponding prices and quantities, we observe that Toyota stands out
as the brand with the highest price, commanding an impressive INR 230,113,130 with 130 units in stock. Following
closely, Maruti Suzuki holds the second position with a price of INR 207,492,800 and 122 units available. Hyundai
also offers a competitive option with a total price of INR 205,002,176 and 112 cars in stock. Tata and Volkswagen
sshare the fourth position with similar quantities, offering 111 cars each priced at INR 197,320,081 and INR 189,602,
158, respectively. Honda, with 110 units in stock, is priced at INR 200,295,418. Mahindra, Kia, Renault, and Ford
complete the list with various price points and quantities, showcasing a diverse range of options for car buyers.
