Locaion Based Car Brands Fuel Type and Revenue Analysis

In this regional analysis of car prices based on fuel type in INR, distinct patterns emerge. Diesel variants, generally
known for their efficiency and torque, tend to command higher prices in Ahmedabad, Gandhinagar, Rajkot, Surat,
and Vadodara, with the highest diesel price observed in Vadodara at INR 216,981,508. In contrast, petrol variants,
often favored for their affordability and smoother performance, present more budget-friendly options, with
the lowest petrol price recorded in Gandhinagar at INR 169,840,883. These regional price variations provide
valuable insights for consumers in Gujarat, enabling them to make informed choices based on their
fuel type preferences and budget considerations.
