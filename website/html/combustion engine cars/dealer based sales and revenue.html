Dealership Based Car Brands Revenue and Sales Volume Analysis

In this analysis of car dealerships, their respective prices, and quantities available, several noteworthy patterns emerge.
Honda Car Center and the Hyundai Dealership both offer competitive pricing and substantial availability, with 210 cars
priced at INR 366,291,959 and 198 cars at INR 372,153,538, respectively, making them attractive choices for buyers.
The Mahindra Showroom provides the broadest range of options, with 242 cars in stock, albeit at a slightly higher price of
INR 430,424,294. The Maruti Suzuki Showroom maintains a strong presence with 226 units at INR 373,651,818, appealing to
those seeking reliability and affordability. Tata Motors Outlet offers 224 cars at INR 389,831,910, providing a diverse selection
within the Tata brand. This analysis underscores the varying choices available, catering to a wide range of preferences
and budgets among car buyers.
