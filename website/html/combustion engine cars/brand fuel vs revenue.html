Car Brands Fuel Type and Revenue Analysis

In this comprehensive analysis of car brands and their corresponding fuel types and prices in INR, several notable trends
emerge. Diesel variants from Honda, Hyundai, Mahindra, Maruti Suzuki, Tata, Toyota, and Volkswagen are generally priced
higher than their petrol counterparts, reflecting the premium associated with diesel engine technology. Among these,
Toyota's diesel models command the highest price at INR 126,035,826, followed closely by Honda at INR 113,566,972. On
the other hand, petrol variants from Ford, Honda, Hyundai, Kia, Mahindra, Maruti Suzuki, Renault, Tata, Toyota, and Volkswagen
offer more budget-friendly options. Among these, Toyota's petrol models stand out with a price of INR 104,077,304. This analysis
provides valuable insights for consumers seeking the right balance between fuel type and pricing within their preferred car brand.
