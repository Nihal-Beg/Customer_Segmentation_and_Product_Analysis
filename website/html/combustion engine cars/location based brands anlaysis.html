Location Based Car Brands Revenue and Sales Volume Analysis

In this regional analysis of car prices, several significant trends emerge. Ahmedabad, with Mahindra, presents
a competitive option with a price of INR 51,907,111. Meanwhile, Gandhinagar boasts Toyota at INR 60,317,085,
reflecting a preference for the brand's premium offerings in the region. Rajkot, with Tata priced at INR 48,640,892,
represents a cost-conscious choice. Surat showcases Hyundai with a price of INR 55,276,006, providing a blend of
affordability and quality. Finally, Vadodara leans towards Honda, with a price tag of INR 59,920,584, reflecting a
preference for the brand's offerings. This regional analysis underscores how different cities in Gujarat gravitate toward
specific car brands, influenced by factors such as pricing and brand reputation.
