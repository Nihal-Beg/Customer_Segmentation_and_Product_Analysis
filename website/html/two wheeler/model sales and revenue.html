Two Wheeler Models Revenue and Sales Volume Analysis

In this analysis of two-wheeler models, their respective prices in INR, and available quantities, we find a diverse range
of options for motorcycle enthusiasts. The Suzuki Gixxer stands out with the highest price at INR 4,034,050, accompanied
by 43 units in stock. The KTM Duke offers a competitive alternative with a price of INR 3,660,034 and 41 units available.
The Suzuki Access and Yamaha R15 provide balanced choices, with prices around INR 3,653,050 and INR 3,537,326,
respectively, and quantities ranging from 38 to 39 units. The Honda CB Shine, Bajaj Pulsar, TVS Jupiter, and Royal Enfield
Classic cater to budget-conscious buyers, offering affordable options with prices ranging from INR 3,321,057 to INR 2,999,657
and varying quantities. This analysis showcases a wide array of two-wheeler models, accommodating different preferences
and budgets within the motorcycle market.
