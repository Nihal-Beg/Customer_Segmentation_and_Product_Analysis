Two Wheeler Models Mileage and Revenue Analysis

In this comprehensive analysis of two-wheeler models, we evaluate both their prices in INR and their respective mileage
in kilometers per liter (km/l), providing valuable insights for potential buyers. The Honda CB Shine emerges as an
economical choice, priced at INR 3,321,057 and delivering an impressive mileage of approximately 63.66 km/l. The Royal
Enfield Bullet and TVS Jupiter follow closely, offering a balance of style and efficiency, with prices around INR 2,812,159
and INR 3,109,751, respectively, and similar mileage figures of approximately 63.54 km/l and 63.30 km/l. The TVS Apache
and KTM Duke present performance-oriented options, with prices near INR 2,861,913 and INR 3,660,034 and mileage
figures of approximately 61.36 km/l and 60.99 km/l. For those seeking scooters, the Honda Activa and Suzuki Access offer
affordability and convenience, with prices around INR 2,021,947 and INR 3,653,050, respectively, and mileage ranging
from 60.91 km/l to 60.87 km/l. The Royal Enfield Classic and Yamaha R15 cater to enthusiasts with prices near INR 2,999,657
and INR 3,537,326 and mileage figures of approximately 59.81 km/l and 59.77 km/l. Lastly, the Bajaj Pulsar, at INR 3,146,901,
combines style with efficiency, offering a mileage of approximately 58.98 km/l. This analysis enables consumers to make
informed decisions based on their preferences for price, mileage, and style within the two-wheeler market.
