Location based Two Wheeler Models Revenue and Sales Volume Analysis

In this regional analysis of two-wheeler models and their respective prices in INR, we observe varying preferences among
different cities in Gujarat. Ahmedabad favors the Suzuki Gixxer, offering it at a price of INR 887,499, catering to those seeking
an economical yet sporty ride. In Gandhinagar, the Yamaha R15 holds prominence with a slightly higher price of INR 1,007,501,
appealing to enthusiasts looking for performance and style. Rajkot showcases the Suzuki Access at INR 1,155,170, providing a
practical and cost-effective option for commuters. Surat, on the other hand, leans toward the KTM Duke, priced at INR 1,124,694,
targeting riders seeking a blend of power and agility. Finally, Vadodara features the Honda Activa at INR 831,374, emphasizing
reliability and convenience for urban transportation. These regional preferences and price dynamics offer valuable insights into
the diverse choices within the two-wheeler market across different cities in Gujarat.
