Location Based Home Appliances Model Type Revenue and sales Volume Analysis

In this regional revenue analysis of home appliances, we observe varying consumer preferences across different cities in Gujarat.
Ahmedabad leads with the highest revenue generated by the Samsung Air Conditioner, totaling 268.2k INR, catering to residents
seeking efficient cooling solutions. In Gandhinagar, the Kenwood Electric Jug stands out with revenue of 205k INR, providing a
convenient option for boiling water. Rajkot follows closely with microwave revenue of 193k INR, emphasizing the importance of
efficient cooking in this city. Surat showcases the popularity of the Pop-Up Toaster with revenue of 181k INR, catering to those
looking for quick breakfast solutions. Vadodara invests in laundry convenience, with Samsung Front Load Washer revenue reaching
192k INR. These revenue figures reflect the diverse consumer demands and priorities within the home appliances market across
these cities in Gujarat.
