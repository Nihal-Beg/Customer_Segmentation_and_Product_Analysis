Location Based Home Appliances Revenue and sales Volume Analysis

In this regional analysis of home appliance types and their corresponding selling prices in INR, we gain insights into
consumer preferences across different cities in Gujarat. Ahmedabad presents the Air Conditioner with a competitive
price of INR 536,158, providing residents with an affordable option for beating the heat. In Gandhinagar, the Mixer
Grinder is available at INR 443,158, catering to households looking for convenience in their kitchen. Rajkot offers the
Air Conditioner at a slightly higher price of INR 554,879, emphasizing cooling solutions for its residents. Surat, known
for its coastal climate, showcases the Induction Cooktop at INR 514,907, offering an efficient cooking option for its
residents. Finally, Vadodara presents the Toaster at INR 450,921, providing a cost-effective solution for quick breakfasts
and snacks. These regional pricing dynamics reflect the varied consumer needs and budget considerations within different
cities in Gujarat's home appliances market.
