Electronics Accessories in Gujarat: Sales Volume and Revneue Analysis

In our analysis of various electronic accessories, including items such as mice, Bluetooth speakers, power banks,
fitness trackers, keyboards, headphones, earbuds, smartwatches, and chargers, we uncovered insights into their
sales performance, original prices, and the resulting revenue generated. Among the accessories, "Headphones"
recorded the highest total sales revenue, amassing an impressive 645,585 units of the local currency. It was closely
followed by "Smartwatch," which generated substantial sales revenue of 574,625 units. "Mouse" and "Bluetooth Speaker"
also demonstrated strong sales performance, contributing sales revenues of 217,135 units and 176,975 units, respectively.
"Power Bank," "Fitness Tracker," "Keyboard," "Earbuds," and "Charger" completed the list of accessories, each displaying varying
sales counts and total revenue figures.

These findings highlight the diverse preferences of consumers when it comes to electronic accessories and underscore the
significance of offering a range of products to cater to different consumer needs. Understanding these trends can assist
businesses in optimizing their product offerings and marketing strategies to enhance sales and profitability in the
competitive electronic accessories market.
