Laptop Model : Feature Popularity Analysis

In our comprehensive analysis of various laptop models, we delved into their sales performance and original prices.
The data provided valuable insights into consumer preferences and the market landscape. The "ROG Zephyrus G14"
laptop model, with 23 units sold, demonstrated strong popularity and generated a total revenue of 2,049.3 INR.
Similarly, the "Vostro 15" and "Legion 5i" models achieved sales counts of 16 and 17 units, respectively, with each generating
significant total revenues of 2,049.3 INR and 2,031.6 INR. The "Creator Z16" laptop model, with 25 units sold, exhibited remarkable
sales performance, accumulating total revenue of 2,030.0 INR. The "Surface Pro 7" and "Ultra 17" models also found their place
in the market, each recording 24 and 29 units sold, resulting in respective total revenues of 2,029.3 INR and 2,022.0 INR. Additional
laptop models, such as the "Galaxy Book S," "VivoBook S15," "ThinkPad X1 Carbon," and "TUF Gaming A15," also contributed to
the market landscape with their respective sales counts and total revenues. These findings illuminate the diverse preferences of
consumers and the performance of various laptop models in the market. Understanding these trends is vital for businesses
aiming to optimize their product offerings and marketing strategies, ensuring they cater effectively to the needs and preferences
of their target audience.
