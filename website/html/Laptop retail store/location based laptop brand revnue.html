Location Based Laptop Brand : Revenue Analysis

In our analysis of laptop sales across different locations, we focused on the performance of a specific laptop brand,
Apple. The data provided insights into the original prices of Apple laptops and their distribution across key cities.
Among the cities analyzed, Ahmedabad, the largest urban center in the region, exhibited robust sales of Apple laptops,
generating a total revenue of 4,291,705 INR from these sales. Similarly, Gandhinagar, with its thriving market, contributed
significantly to the brand's sales, amassing a total revenue of 5,742,966 INR. Rajkot demonstrated its appetite for Apple
laptops, resulting in total revenue of 5,157,023 INR. Surat, another key city in the analysis, featured Apple laptop sales that
generated a total revenue of 2,826,726 INR. Vadodara, rounding up the list of influential cities, recorded total revenue
of 3,852,148 INR from the sale of Apple laptops.
