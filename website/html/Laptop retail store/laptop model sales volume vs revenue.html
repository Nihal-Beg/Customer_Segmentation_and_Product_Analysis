Top Laptop Models in Gujarat: Sales Volume and Revenue Analysis

In our analysis of laptop sales, we examined the performance of various laptop models based on their original prices.
Notably, the "MacBook Pro 16" emerged as the top-priced model with an original price of ₹8,397,532. This high-end
offering is followed by the "MacBook Pro 13" and the "MacBook Air," which exhibit original prices of ₹6,844,539 and
₹6,628,497, respectively. On the Windows laptop front, the "Galaxy Book Flex" and "Galaxy Book S" stand as competitive
options with original prices of ₹4,728,738 and ₹4,134,830 , respectively. These findings provide valuable insights into the
laptop market, showcasing the diversity of options available to consumers.
