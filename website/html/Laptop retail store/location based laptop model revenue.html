Location Based Laptop Model : Revenue Analysis

In our analysis of laptop sales across various locations and laptop models, we gained valuable insights into consumer
preferences and product choices. The dataset provided information on the original prices of different laptop models sold
in key cities. Ahmedabad, a significant urban center, displayed a notable interest in the "MacBook Pro 16" model, resulting
in a total sales revenue of 1,983,557 INR. Similarly, Gandhinagar exhibited a strong preference for the same model,
generating a total revenue of 2,352,528 INR from its sales. Rajkot, another influential city in our analysis, favored the
"MacBook Pro 13," amassing a total revenue of 2,044,707 INR. Surat, a key player in the laptop market, leaned towards the
"Modern 14" model, with total sales revenue of 1,186,088 INR. Vadodara contributed to the diversity of laptop choices, with
another affinity for the "MacBook Pro 16," resulting in total sales revenue of 1,808,373 INR.

These insights underscore the regional variations in laptop model preferences and their respective sales performance across
different cities. Understanding these patterns is crucial for businesses looking to tailor their product offerings and marketing
strategies to effectively target specific consumer segments in each location, ultimately optimizing sales and market share.
