Laptop Brands in Gujarat: Price Vs Sales Analysis

In our extensive Price vs Sales analysis, we explored the intricate interplay between product pricing
and sales volume. The dataset offered valuable insights into market dynamics, revealing a mean price
of 114,812.007 units of the local currency and an average sales volume of 16,169.516 units. These
figures provided a broad overview of the pricing and sales landscape.

Additionally, we examined the central tendencies in the data, finding that the median price and
median sales figures were 110,001.0 and 16,180.5 units, respectively. These medians highlighted
the typical values in our dataset. However, it was the wide-ranging data that caught our attention.
The dataset featured a minimum price of 33,930 units and a minimum sales volume of just 77 units,
indicating the presence of lower-priced products with limited sales. In contrast, the maximum price
reached a substantial high of 285,521 units, with the maximum sales volume impressively peaking at 49,847 units.
