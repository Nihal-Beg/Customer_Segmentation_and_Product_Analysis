Laptop Model Featrue Popularity Revenue and Processor Analysis

In our analysis of laptop processors and their associated original prices, we gained insights into the market's processor
preferences and their corresponding price points. The data revealed that the "AMD Ryzen 5" processor, while being a
popular choice, had an original price of 21,765,340 INR. Similarly, the "AMD Ryzen 7" processor, known for its performance,
was priced at 21,062,381 INR. On the Intel side, the "Intel Core i3" processor exhibited a competitive price of 23,296,574 INR,
while the "Intel Core i5" and "Intel Core i7" processors were priced at 24,251,189 INR and 24,436,523 INR, respectively.

These findings showcase the variations in processor pricing within the market, reflecting both consumer preferences and
performance considerations. Understanding the pricing dynamics of different processors is essential for businesses to
cater effectively to their target audience and make informed decisions regarding product offerings and marketing strategies.
