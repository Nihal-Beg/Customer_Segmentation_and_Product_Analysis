Location Based Laptop Model : Sales Volume Analysis

In our analysis of laptop sales across different locations and laptop brands, we uncovered intriguing patterns in consumer
preferences and product popularity. The data provided insights into the sales of various laptop models in key cities.
Ahmedabad, a bustling urban center, exhibited a penchant for the "Galaxy Book Flex" laptop model, with a total of 11 units
sold. In Gandhinagar, the "Gram 14" laptop model emerged as a favored choice, with 12 units sold. Rajkot, another significant
city in our analysis, displayed a preference for the "MacBook Air," resulting in 10 units sold. Surat, a key player in the market,
also leaned towards the "Gram 14" model, with 12 units sold.

Vadodara contributed to the diversity of laptop choices, with a strong preference for the "Galaxy Book S," recording a total of
10 units sold. These insights underscore the regional variations in laptop model preferences and sales across different cities.
Understanding these patterns is essential for businesses looking to tailor their product offerings and marketing strategies to
effectively target specific consumer segments in each location, ultimately optimizing sales and market share.
