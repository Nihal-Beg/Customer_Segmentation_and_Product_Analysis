Electric Cars Range and Sales Volume Analysis

In this analysis of electric car models, their respective mileage in kilometers per liter (km/l), and available quantities, we
observe a variety of choices for environmentally conscious consumers. The Hyundai Kona Electric and MG ZS EV offer
competitive options with mileage figures of approximately 213.15 km/l and 196.79 km/l, respectively, aligning with
efficiency and sustainability. The Mahindra eKUV100, Tata Altroz EV, Tata Nexon EV, and Tata Tigor EV provide alternatives
with mileage ranging from approximately 195.81 km/l to 206.17 km/l, appealing to those seeking both eco-friendliness
and value. These figures, combined with the quantity available for each model, showcase a range of electric vehicles catering
to diverse preferences and needs within the electric car market.
