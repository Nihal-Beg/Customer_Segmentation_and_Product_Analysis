Location Based Top Selling Electric Car Sales Volume and Revenue Analysis

In this regional analysis of electric car models and their respective prices in INR, we observe varying preferences among
different cities in Gujarat. Ahmedabad showcases the Tata Nexon EV with a price of INR 37,100,444, while Gandhinagar
values the same model slightly higher at INR 43,789,878, indicating regional price variations. Rajkot offers the Tata Nexon EV
at a more affordable INR 30,537,094, catering to budget-conscious consumers. Surat, on the other hand, favors the
Mahindra eKUV100, priced at INR 47,245,855. Vadodara aligns with Ahmedabad's choice, offering the Tata Nexon EV at
INR 39,795,609. This analysis highlights how regional preferences and pricing dynamics influence electric car choices
in Gujarat's different cities.
