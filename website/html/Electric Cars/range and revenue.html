Electric Cars Range and Revenue Analysis

In this comprehensive analysis of electric car models, we assess their efficiency in terms of mileage (measured in
kilometers per liter, km/l) alongside their corresponding prices in INR. The Hyundai Kona Electric stands out as
the most efficient, offering an impressive mileage of approximately 213.15 km/l, paired with a price of INR
170,553,910. The MG ZS EV follows closely with a mileage of approximately 196.79 km/l and a price of INR 171,162,740.
The Mahindra eKUV100 and Tata Altroz EV provide more budget-friendly options, delivering mileage figures of around
198.23 km/l and 200.80 km/l, respectively, paired with prices of INR 140,307,952 and INR 140,037,840. The Tata Nexon EV,
with a mileage of about 195.81 km/l, represents a premium choice, priced at INR 180,284,595, while the Tata Tigor EV
combines a commendable mileage of approximately 206.17 km/l with a price tag of INR 156,537,138. This analysis offers
valuable insights into the trade-offs between efficiency and pricing within the electric car market, catering to a diverse
range of consumer preferences and budgets.
