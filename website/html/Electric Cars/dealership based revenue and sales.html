Dealership Based Top Selling Electric Car Sales Volume and Revenue Analysis

In this analysis of electric car models and their respective fuel types and prices in INR, we observe a diverse range of options
catering to environmentally conscious consumers. The Hyundai Kona Electric and MG ZS EV present competitive choices,
priced at INR 170,553,910 and INR 171,162,740, respectively. Meanwhile, the Mahindra eKUV100 and Tata Altroz EV offer
more budget-friendly options, priced at INR 140,307,952 and INR 140,037,840. The Tata Nexon EV commands a premium
price at INR 180,284,595, while the Tata Tigor EV falls in between, priced at INR 156,537,138. This analysis showcases a variety
of electric car models, accommodating different budgets and preferences among potential buyers in the electric vehicle market.
