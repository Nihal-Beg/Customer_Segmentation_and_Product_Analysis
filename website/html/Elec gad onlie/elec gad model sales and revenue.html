Electronic Accessories E-commerce: Sales Volume and Revenue Analysis

In this analysis of specific gadget models, we observe a range of original prices and quantities available in the Indian market.
True Wireless Earbuds, with an average price of 543,591 INR, are the most numerous among these models, with 84 units in stock,
indicating their popularity among consumers. Noise-Canceling Headphones, priced at 685,112 INR, maintain a moderate quantity
of 54 units, reflecting their demand among those seeking a superior audio experience. GPS Fitness Watches offer an affordable
option, averaging 158,588 INR, and come in 53 units, making them an attractive choice for fitness enthusiasts. Fitness Smartwatches,
with an average price of 672,016 INR, are available in 52 units, suggesting their appeal to those seeking a blend of style and fitness
functionality. Optical Mice, priced at 159,226 INR, are available in 48 units, catering to consumers in need of reliable computer
peripherals. This analysis provides valuable insights into the preferences of consumers for specific gadget models, highlighting the
variety of options available to cater to different needs and budgets in the Indian market.
