Electronic Accessories E-commerce: Sales Volume and Revenue Analysis

In this analysis of various gadget types, we observe both differences in original prices and quantities available in the Indian market.
Fitness trackers, with an average price of 592,734 INR, have the highest quantity of 196 units, suggesting their popularity among consumers.
Headphones, on the other hand, command a substantially higher average price of 2,221,198 INR but still maintain a considerable quantity
of 180 units, underlining their significance in the gadget market. Power banks and smartwatches offer more affordable options, with average
prices of 556,936 INR and 2,053,429 INR, respectively, and both share the same quantity of 176 units. Bluetooth speakers and chargers
are priced similarly, with quantities of 163 and 162 units, respectively. Earbuds, with an average price of 439,856 INR, offer an attractive
option to consumers and are available in 152 units. Lastly, keyboards and mice, priced at 464,421 INR and 437,485 INR, respectively,
are also popular choices, with quantities of 148 and 147 units. This analysis provides insights into the gadget preferences of consumers,
with fitness trackers, headphones, and smartwatches being prominent choices, while also emphasizing the importance of offering
a range of price options in the competitive gadget market.
