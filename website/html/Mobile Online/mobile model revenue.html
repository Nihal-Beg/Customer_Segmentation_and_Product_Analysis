Top Mobile Model E-Commerce : Revenue Analysis

In this analysis of mobile phone sales, the iPhone SE emerges as the top-selling model, generating an impressive
4,053,615.00 INR in revenue. It is followed closely by the iPhone 12 Mini, which raked in 3,980,487.15 INR, and
the iPhone 12, with sales totaling 3,717,832.50 INR. The iPhone XR also performed well, contributing 3,503,298.07
INR to the overall revenue. Among other notable models are the Nokia C20, Google Pixel 4a, Google Pixel 5a, and
Nord CE 5G, all making significant contributions to the sales figures. This analysis highlights the popularity of Apple's
iPhone models in the market and underscores the diverse range of choices available to consumers, with both
budget-friendly and premium options capturing attention in the mobile phone landscape.
