Amazon: Revenue Vs Review Analysis

The correlation coefficient of 0.0173 between price and review on Amazon suggests a very weak positive correlation
between product prices and customer review ratings on Amazon. This implies that while there is a slight tendency
for higher-priced products to receive slightly higher review ratings, the relationship is practically insignificant.
Various other factors, such as product quality, features, and customer experiences, are likely more influential in
determining the review ratings on Amazon.Therefore, when assessing the impact of price on reviews, it becomes
evident that Amazon customers' evaluations are driven by multiple factors beyond just the cost of the product.
