Top Selling Mobile Model Comparison Revenue Analysis (Amazon & Flipkart)

The analysis delves deeper into the revenue generated by specific mobile phone models on both Amazon and Flipkart,
revealing intriguing insights. At the forefront, the iPhone Mini shines with stellar performance, raking in an impressive
2.44 million INR on Amazon and 1.5 million INR on Flipkart, showcasing its desirability among consumers. Following closely,
the iPhone SE exhibits its financial prowess, accumulating 2.2 million INR on Amazon and 1.7 million INR on Flipkart.
The iPhone 12 also cements its position in the market, yielding 1.9 million INR on Amazon and 1.7 million INR on Flipkart.
